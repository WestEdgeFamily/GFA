Understanding Ingredient Results

Tips for Celiac Safety:

  • Wheat derivatives - Look for ingredients like modified food starch, maltodextrin, and caramel color, which may contain gluten depending on their source.
  • Hidden sources - Be aware that "natural flavors" and "spices" can sometimes contain gluten-derived ingredients.
  • Cross-contamination - Products manufactured on shared equipment may have cross-contamination risks not reflected in the ingredient list.
  • Processing aids - Some ingredients used in processing may not be listed but could contain gluten.
  • Regional differences - Ingredients like "glucose syrup" may be gluten-derived in some countries but corn-derived in others.

Important: This tool provides guidance but cannot replace medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider with concerns.
