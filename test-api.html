Gluten-Free Scanner

Welcome to the Gluten-Free Scanner

This tool helps people with celiac disease and gluten sensitivity quickly analyze food ingredient lists to identify potential gluten sources. Simply upload a photo of an ingredients label or type the ingredients text to get an instant safety assessment.

Photo Scanning

Take a photo of any ingredient list and our AI will automatically find, crop, and analyze the ingredients.

Ingredient Analysis

Our specialized algorithm identifies gluten-containing ingredients and hidden sources.

Safety Assessment

Get clear safety ratings with detailed explanations of any flagged ingredients.

How to Use This Tool

1

Take a Photo

Snap a picture of an ingredient list using your camera or upload one from your gallery.

2

AI Processing

Our system will automatically detect the ingredients text, extract it, and analyze for gluten content.

3

Review Results

Check the detailed analysis to make informed decisions about the product's safety.

Important: Always consult with your healthcare provider for medical advice. This tool is designed as a helpful guide but cannot replace professional medical guidance.
